# Grant Alignment

## UCE + Goose
UCE complements Goose by providing deterministic impact analysis and governance checks before actions are executed. Goose remains the execution agent; UCE becomes the validation layer.

## MCP Ecosystem Value
UCE adds a deterministic reasoning toolchain to MCP, enabling structured policy and requirement enforcement with traceable outputs.

## Why Deterministic Grounding Matters
Enterprises require reproducibility, auditability, and explainable reasoning. Probabilistic retrieval systems cannot guarantee these properties.

## Enterprise Relevance
UCE reduces unsafe changes by quantifying impact across schema, code, and governance layers. This supports compliance workflows and change-management protocols.

## Open-Source Benefit
Open release allows community validation, extension, and integration with diverse codebases, improving transparency and accelerating adoption.
