ID: P-001
Title: Meeting Data Audit Integrity Policy
Type: Internal / SOC2 CC7.2
Description: Meeting data must preserve audit integrity at all times. Any modification to core meeting fields (status, user_id, agent_id, created_at) must be recorded in an append-only audit log. The audit log must capture the actor identity, timestamp, before/after state, and the reason for change where available.
Controls:
  - C1: Database trigger or ORM lifecycle hook records every UPDATE/DELETE on the `meetings` table.
  - C2: The `created_at` column is immutable after INSERT (enforced by DB constraint or application guard).
  - C3: Audit log rows are written to a separate schema/table not accessible by the application's write role.
  - C4: Audit log retention is a minimum of 12 months (SOC2 requirement).
Enforces: RQ-001, RQ-003
Enforcement Mode: block
GDPR Relevance: Audit logs contain personal data (user_id, agent_id, timestamps). They must be scoped to Art. 6(1)(c) legal obligation or Art. 6(1)(f) legitimate interest and MUST be excluded from Art. 17 erasure (retain for legal/compliance reasons under Art. 17(3)(b)).
Review Date: 2026-12-01
Approver: Data Protection Officer
