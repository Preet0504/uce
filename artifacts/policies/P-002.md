ID: P-002
Title: Recording and Transcript Retention Policy
Type: GDPR Art. 5(1)(e) / Internal
Description: Recorded meeting artifacts (audio recordings, transcripts, summary text) must remain accessible only for the minimum period necessary for the stated processing purpose. Recordings stored beyond the retention window must be automatically purged. Any code change that extends retention, copies artifacts to new storage, or adds new processing of transcript content requires a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) review.
Controls:
  - C1: Retention period is defined per deployment in `uce.config.yaml` under `data_retention.recordings_days` (default 90).
  - C2: A scheduled job runs daily to delete `recording_url` / `transcript_url` content and null the DB columns for meetings whose `ended_at` is older than the retention period.
  - C3: `transcript_url` storage bucket has server-side encryption (SSE-S3 or equivalent) enabled.
  - C4: Any sharing of transcript content with a third-party processor requires a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).
Enforces: RQ-002, RQ-009
Enforcement Mode: advisory
GDPR Relevance: Audio transcripts may capture health, political, or religious opinions (Art. 9 special-category data). The retention period is a core data-minimisation obligation under Art. 5(1)(e). Violation of this policy constitutes a reportable breach risk under Art. 33.
Review Date: 2026-12-01
Approver: Data Protection Officer
