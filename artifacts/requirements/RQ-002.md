ID: RQ-002
Title: Persist meeting recordings and transcripts
Priority: Medium
Description: The `meetings` table MUST store `recording_url` and `transcript_url` to keep recorded artifacts accessible for the full retention period. These URLs must point to durable storage (e.g. object-store pre-signed paths with a minimum TTL of 30 days) and MUST be invalidated upon meeting deletion or user erasure request.
Acceptance Criteria:
  - `recording_url` and `transcript_url` accept nullable values (some meetings may not be recorded).
  - When a recording exists, the URL resolves to a valid resource within the storage TTL window.
  - Deletion of a meeting record triggers a corresponding object-store cleanup job (async OK, but must complete within 24 h).
  - Transcript content is treated as personal data (voice/text of participants); storage must be encrypted at rest.
Tags: recordings, storage, retention
GDPR Note: Audio and textual transcripts are personal data (Art. 4(1)) and may include special-category data (e.g. health information mentioned in conversation). Retention period MUST be defined in the DPIA; default maximum is 90 days unless a longer period is contractually required and documented.
